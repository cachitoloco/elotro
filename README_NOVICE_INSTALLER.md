## 🧠 **How Smart Volatility Helps Beginners**

### **🎯 AI Adapts Automatically:**

**Normal Market:**
- 15% dip → Small buy (0.05 WLD)
- 5% profit → Sells at exactly 5%

**Volatile Market:**
