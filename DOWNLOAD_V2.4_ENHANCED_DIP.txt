🎯 NEW STRATEGY CREATION WORKFLOW:

1. Select Strategy Builder from main menu
2. Choose "Create New Strategy"
3. Select trading pair (ORO, YIELD, Ramen, or discovered tokens)
4. Set DIP threshold % (e.g., 10% for 10% drop)
5. Set profit target % (e.g., 5% for 5% gain)
6. Choose DIP timeframe:
   • 1 minute (fast, volatile tokens)
   • 5 minutes (balanced, recommended)  
   • 15 minutes (stable tokens)
   • 1 hour (long-term DIP detection)
   • Custom (specify your own)
7. Enable historical analysis (optional)
8. Start strategy and monitor enhanced status updates
